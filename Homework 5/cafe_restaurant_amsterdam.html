Cafe Restaurant Amsterdam


In the former, gigantic, Machinepump building near the Westerpark in Amsterdam, you have been able to enjoy a delicious lunch and dinner at Café Restaurant Amsterdam for years. Amsterdammers used to buy soft dune water here for a penny, but now you can enjoy up to 20 (!) different desserts at this impressive location.
